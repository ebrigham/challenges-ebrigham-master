Black Bottomr Cupcakes

From: Taste of Home Fall Baking 2016


Chocolate cupcakes topped with a cream cheese filling. Delicious!

Makes 20-24 cupcakes.


Ingredients:

  • 1 8 oz. package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/3 cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 + 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 Tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/4 cup baking cocoa
  • 1 tsp baking soda

Method:

  1. 1. In a small bowl, beat the cream cheese, 1/3 cup of sugar, 1 egg, and 1/8 tsp salt until smooth. Stir in chips; set aside.
  2. 2. For cupcakes, in a large bowl, beat 1 cup sugar, water, oil, 1 egg, vinegar, and vanilla until well blended. Combine the flour, cocoa, baking soda, and 1/2 tsp salt; gradually beat into the egg mixture until blended.
  3. 3. Fill paper-lined muffin -cups half full with chocolate batter. Drop a heaping tablespoonful of cheese mixture in center of batter of each cupcake.
  4. 4. Bake at 350° for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the cake portion comes out clean. Cool in pans for 10 minutes before removing to racks to cool completely. Refrigerate the leftovers.
